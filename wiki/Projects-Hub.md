# Official Plugins

### ExtractorPlugin

> This plugin can extract the info, search, and play songs directly from its source.

- [@DisTune/youtube](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@DisTune/youtube): Support YouTube.
- [@DisTune/soundcloud](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@DisTune/soundcloud): Support SoundCloud.

### InfoExtractorPlugin

> This plugin only can extract the info from supported links, but not play songs directly from its source.

- [@DisTune/spotify](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@DisTune/spotify): Support Spotify.
- [@DisTune/deezer](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@DisTune/deezer): Support Deezer.

### PlayableExtractorPlugin

> This plugin can extract and play songs from supported links, but cannot search for songs from its source.

- [@DisTune/yt-dlp](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@DisTune/yt-dlp): Use `yt-dlp` for supporting 700+ sites.
- [@DisTune/direct-link](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@DisTune/direct-link): Support direct audio links.
- [@DisTune/file](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@DisTune/file): Support local files.
- [@DisTune/bandlab](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@DisTune/bandlab): Support BandLab.
